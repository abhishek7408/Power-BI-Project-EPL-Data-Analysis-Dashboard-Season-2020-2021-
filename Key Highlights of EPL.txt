started on September 12, 2020, and ended on May 23, 2021. Manchester City were crowned champions for the fifth time, finishing 12 points 
clear of second-placed Manchester United. They won 27 of their 38 league matches, scoring 82 goals and conceding only 32. The top goal 
scorer of the season was Harry Kane of Tottenham Hotspur with 23 goals, followed by Mohamed Salah of Liverpool with 22 goals. The player 
with the most assists was Harry Kane with 14 assists.
